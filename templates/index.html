Fake Image Detection System

About Our Approach

Our system uses three complementary approaches to detect fake images:

  • Bilateral High-Pass Filter (BiHPF): Analyzes frequency domain characteristics of images to detect manipulation.
  • Capsule Network: Focuses on spatial domain analysis using advanced neural network architecture.
  • Keras Model: A general-purpose neural network trained for image authenticity detection.
